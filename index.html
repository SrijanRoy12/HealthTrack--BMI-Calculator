About BMI

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It helps categorize whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

BMI Categories

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal weight: BMI 18.5-24.9
  • Overweight: BMI 25-29.9
  • Obese: BMI 30 or greater
